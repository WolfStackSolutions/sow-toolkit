SOW Assistant Tools v5 Service Operations Workspace

Install

Drag the button to your bookmarks bar, then click it on any SOW page to open the toolkit menu. Clicking it on webapp.brightmetrics.com starts the BM keep-alive panel instead. Press Ctrl+Shift+B if the bookmarks bar is hidden.

SOW Toolkit drag it to your bookmarks bar

Tools

15 modules
  1. as Alert Suppressor V5 Blocks notification banners and prevents them from stealing your focus while typing.
  2. tc Ticket Copy Click any ticket tab to copy the ticket number to your clipboard.
  3. tabcnt Tab Counter Shows how many tabs you have open in SOW.
  4. bgopen Background Open Shift+click a ticket link to open it as a workspace tab without leaving the page you are on. Works from any page, router-level, no focus bounce.
  5. tlt Tab Load Time Displays how long each ticket tab took to load.
  6. lc Lockout Check Quickly check if an account is locked out in AD.
  7. gt Genesys Timer Live call timer, idle tracker, wrap-up countdown, and daily call stats.
  8. ad Attachment Drop V2 Drag and drop files anywhere on a ticket to attach them. Includes rename and confirm.
  9. lbt Extend Max Tab Limit Raises the workspace tab cap past the stock 10, with an editable limit in the menu (default 16). Sub tab cap scales with it. Lives in the Limit Break box.
  10. lbc Tab Cache Persist Bypass Major performance increase. Unlocks SOW's screen keep-alive pool (stock config keeps only 3 active / 5 cached, way below vendor defaults) so tabs stay loaded instead of rebuilding for 5 seconds on switch. Pool scales with your tab limit. Chart button opens a live monitor: heap, mounted pages, long tasks, re-asserts.
  11. tb Tooltip Blocker off by default Blocks those dumb tooltips that get in the way, yk the ones im talking about.
  12. qa Quick Associate Adds an inline box next to Create incident on IMS records. Type an INC or REQ and hit Save to link it instantly, no modal.
  13. ci Caller Insight Injects caller history into IMS contact cards. Shows recent incidents, requests, and interactions with a neon pulse on today's activity.
  14. ts Ticket Search Adds a search bar to the header. Drop down your last 50 incidents, requests, and interactions, filter as you type, or search all of yours. Click to open in place.
  15. tt The Better Watchlist Watches tickets you opened or picked up (pick-up excludes IMS), incl. SCTASKs with RITM/SCTASK family linking. Toasts on group and assignee changes, state moves, resolutions with close notes, reopens, and customer comments, even after close.
  16. bmka BM Keep Alive Runs automatically when you click the bookmarklet on webapp.brightmetrics.com instead of the SOW menu. Keeps your Brightmetrics session and board refreshing while the tab is hidden (visibility spoof, anti-throttle, activity nudges) with a draggable stats panel and live log.
